Privacy Policy

We take our user's data privacy seriously and such, our app only do necessary data collection.

The application gather the user's email address as part of the account creation process and to prevent misuse. A user id will also be generated as part of the account's identification. AAID will also be collected for advertising purposes. Phone numbers will be also gathered for temporarily record keeping of message transaction and adding the number to blocked list (user's won't be able to send text message to blocked numbers). IP Address of the message sender will be also collected to prevent misuse of the service. SMS message will also be stored temporarily for record keeping. All temporary data will be deleted after a few month(s).

Even we collect some data especially the one generated from sending anonymous SMS (to prevent misuse) through the app, the user can still assure that they are anonymous since their recipient won't be able to know who is the sender. However, request from legal actions are exempted to this anonymity.

The app also uses third party ad network for its ads. If you wish to opt out for the data collection done by the third party, then you will need to uninstall the app fully. The following are the ad networks the app is using:
