Privacy Policy

We take our user's data privacy seriously and such , our app only do minimum data collection. The application collects data mostly through personalized ads and firebase analytics. If you wish to opt out with this data collection , then you need to uninstall the application fully. We can't delete the data collected by the two mentioned because it's managed by our third party partners.

The application needs to access internet and the storage. Internet permission are used for ads and fetching updated data from the server. Storage permission is for exporting your created cards.
