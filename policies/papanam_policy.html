Privacy Policy

We take our user's data privacy seriously and such, our app only do necessary data collection.

The third party collects data from our application by personalized ads. We use analytics to gather necessary data from the users (errors caused by the user in the app and element interactions) to improve the application. If you wish to opt out with this data collections, then you need to uninstall the application fully. We can't delete the data collected by the third party because it's managed by them.

The app collects necessary data in order for the system to function such as precise and approximate location, email addresses upon account creation, and messages sent using the message functionality of the application. All messages processesed in the application are using end to end encryption. The end to end encryption ensures that messages upon sending, transmitting, receiving and storing of the messages in our database are in encrypted form. To simplify, no one can read messages except for the sender and receiver. The data collected not by the third party can be deleted from our database, just email us - help.leafyfied@gmail.com
