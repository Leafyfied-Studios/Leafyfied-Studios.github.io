Privacy Policy

We take our user's data privacy seriously and such , our app only do minimum data collection. The application collects data mostly through personalized ads and firebase analytics. If you wish to opt out with this data collection , then you need to uninstall the application fully. We , the developers , does not collect any private data from the users (except for email addresses from account creation and mobile number for Gcash payouts stored in Firestore). You can always request for an account and email deletion from us.
