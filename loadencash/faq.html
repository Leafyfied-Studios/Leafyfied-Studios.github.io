Frequently Ask Questions

How do I contact you ?
You can emails us at loadencash@gmail.com or message us at facebook.

What's the minimum and maximum selling of load?
Minimum is ₱20. Maximum depends on the current time. You can see the available load you can sell at "SELL LOAD" tab.

What's the minimum and maximum buying of load?
Minimum is ₱20. Maximum depends on the current time. Message us to know the maximum load you can buy at the current time.

How long will it take to receive the payment from selling load ?
It depends. Usually few hours to one day. However, it will take few days sometimes.

Can I refund the load that I have already sent ?
Yes, message immediately. However it will cost you ₱2 load. So if you ask for a refund of ₱20 load, you will only receive ₱18 load. Please take note also that we won't be giving refund if we had already paid you out.

I didn't receive my payment, what to do ?
Double check everything. If you are really sure you don't receive the payment, then contact us immediately.

I accidentally entered the incorrect details, what to do ?
Message us immediately. Take note that intentionally putting wrong details to trick us will lead to account suspension.
